Need

"This convergence of forces—growing cancer and survival rates, oncology shortages, and gaps in primary care training—lead directly to what we call the Survivorship Gap. And to solve the Survivorship Gap, we need new, innovative, and patient-centered models to deliver survivorship care, at scale, to tens of millions of survivors. So that they, too, do not fall into 'the gap'" – Hil Moss, VivorCare Co-Founder & CEO

Mission

“Creating a model for survivorship care that scales to support tens of millions of survivors. A model that mitigates disparities and increases access to survivorship care — not the other way around. A model designed by, with, and for survivors. That’s what we’re building at VivorCare.” – Hil Moss, VivorCare Co-Founder & CEO

Value Proposition

VivorCare is a pioneering virtual clinic that caters to individuals who have experienced cancer. They offer comprehensive assistance throughout your survivorship journey, equipping you with the necessary resources and encouragement to navigate this challenging phase of your life.

3 Pillars of Care

Hover over each image to view the pillar!

Care Team

A virtual Care Team consisting of a Survivorship Clinician, Therapist, and Peer Coach who are well-versed in the unique difficulties of survivorship and are dedicated to providing assistance

Care Plan

A personalized Survivorship Care Plan that is tailored to your individual needs, helping you manage their ongoing care, monitoring your progress, and promoting your overall well-being

Care Coordination

Facilitated continuous communication and collaboration among your oncology team, primary care physician, and other specialists involved in your care to ensure that everyone is informed and working towards your survivorship objectives

What sparked my interest...Meet Michelle

When I was younger, I called Michelle my second mom. When I was in 5th grade, Michelle was diagnosed with Breast Cancer. It has been years since I cut off and donated my ponytail to support Michelle. I do not see her often anymore, but I do wonder how she is doing. I wonder if she worries about the cancer coming back. I have learned that cancer care continues long after diagnosis and remission. Thinking about Michelle brought me to VivorCare whose mission is to make care accessible to the millions of people like Michelle who survived Cancer.

Leah Feilbogen - Moonshot Company Scouting
