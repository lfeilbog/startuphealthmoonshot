Mission

“Mili believes in a patient-centered approach with convenient access that empowers you to be proactive about your health. Whether at work, at home, or even in the infusion suite, our experts are just a video-call away. Mili was founded by a physician-led team that knows how difficult it is for patients to access the support they need, when they need it, from people they can trust. Mili is a modern take on oncology support that meets you where you are. Cancer is hard, but getting the support you need is easy with Mili. Come thrive with us.” – Mili

Value Proposition

Mili is a virtual platform that provides a comprehensive range of support services to individuals who are battling cancer or are cancer survivors. With a team of licensed professionals who specialize in cancer-focused nutrition, mental health, and wellness services, Mili provides clients easy access to the necessary support and guidance to optimize cancer patients and survivors’ health and wellbeing, all within a virtual setting. By leveraging digital technologies and innovative virtual care strategies, Mili empowers cancer patients and survivors to lead fulfilling and healthy lives by addressing their unique needs and challenges.

What sparked my interest...Meet Pam

Learn more about Pam's story here

Everyone in my town loves Pam! Regardless of whether you need a favor or a friend, Pam is always there to help everyone. And then, out of nowhere, Pam was diagnosed with Stage 3 Lobular Breast Cancer. The community that loved her so much wanted to help. We cooked meals and sent cards, but Pam needed help that we did not know how to provide. That made me think of Mili, an easily accessible online platform that could sensitively address Pam’s emotional concerns, her struggle to find healthy foods to eat, and the difficulty she had continuing to exercise. Mili could be the support that we all wished we could be.

Leah Feilbogen - Moonshot Company Scouting
