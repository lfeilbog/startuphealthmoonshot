Need

“Today it's estimated that only 2% of the population participates in clinical research due to a lack of awareness and access” – BuildClinical

Mission

“We believe it shouldn’t be a challenge for people to find research studies that they can benefit from. Our vision is to enable the community to have access to all clinical research opportunities and for researchers to easily engage their ideal participants. BuildClinical’s goal is to bridge the communication gap that exists between clinical researchers and the community by utilizing technology” – BuildClinical

Value Proposition

BuildClinical’s services enables researchers to connect with individuals who may benefit from their studies. Through their platform, academic researchers can expedite their clinical research patient recruitment efforts by utilizing digital advertising to reach the specific population required, minimizing the existing gaps in patient access and awareness. Additionally, patients can gain access to new clinical trials.

What sparked my interest... Meet Talia

When Talia was first diagnosed with migraine headaches at age 16, a trip to the ER for a “Migraine Cocktail" IV broke the headache, and she was on her way. Over the years, her migraines have increased in both intensity and duration, and now my fun-loving sister can be found sitting on the sidelines of the life she loves to live. Easy access to clinical studies to reduce migraine pain could greatly improve Talia's quality of life. This wish for my sister brought me to BuildClinical. I can only imagine the medical advancements that might occur as BuildClinical increases patients’ awareness and accessibility to clinical trials and helps doctors find required sample sets in order to conduct their research.

Leah Feilbogen - Moonshot Company Scouting
